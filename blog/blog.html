[parent_date] | Why and how a Roguelike Metroidvania could work

Roguelike and Metroidvania are polar opposite genres. It's true, just look at them!

In a metroidvania game everything has to be designed very intricately, so that nothing would feel out of place, and nothing could even in theory lock you out of progression.

A roguelike, on the other hand, focuses on randomly generating both challenges and powerups, so things don't feel predetermined.

But even despite all the differences, a roguelike metroidvania could still exist. We just need to look at the similarities.
