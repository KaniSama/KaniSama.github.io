Terms of Service

1. Pay upfront and in full. Payment through boosty.to only (you can use PayPal or debit card).
2. No refunds for any reason. I wouldn't be able to refund you your money even if I tried.
3. I retain all rights to my art. Your commission may appear on my social media pages, unless a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) is discussed beforehand.
4. I have the right to refuse your commission request without stating the reason. Though I will probably state it anyway.
5. You may not resell my art or make prints of it for sale, unless we specifically agreed upon it.
6. You may use my art in non-commercial projects, given you provide proper credit. Please notify me when using it. Usage in small-scale commercial projects is negotiable.
7. You may not use my art for AI training, anything related to NFTs, or any even remotely adjacent purpose.
8. Refer to Examples and Pricing to see if your request is ok or not. I may not be able to draw what you want me to draw.
9. Completion time for an artwork is 2 to 4 weeks, depending on complexity and how many commissions I'm working at the time.
10. You are entitled to one free revision. Further revisions will add 10% each to the overall cost of the commission.

Payment Info

Because of the limitations imposed on my country, the only way to pay me directly is through boosty.to.
It accepts Paypal or debit card, but you'll have to create a boosty account.
It's pretty quick, and hey! Unlike a direct Paypal transfer, this way I won't know any of your personal information!

How To Order

Read the Terms of Service and OK and Not OK sections.

DM me on Discord Naisy#6058 or on Twitter @knkeien. Lay out your awesome idea. State your budget if possible.
Provide reference for the idea/character. It can be a drawing, a stick figure, a text description, – just anything to make my understanding of the character and the scene more accurate.

After we agree on the price, pay the full amount using the Goals section on my boosty
(You will need to create a boosty.to account, as explained in the Payment Info section).

Confirm your payment with me. I will start drawing and keep you updated.
During the sketching phase, you can ask me to adjust the sketch any number of times, but when the sketch is completed, you only have 1 free revision, as stated in the Terms of Service.

Completion time 2 to 4 weeks.
