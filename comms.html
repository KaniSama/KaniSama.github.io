Payment Info

Because of the limitations imposed on my country, it's impossible to pay me directly. Thankfully, you can still pay me through boosty.to. You can still use Paypal or your debit card, but you'll have to create a boosty account.
It's pretty quick, and hey! Unlike a direct Paypal transfer, this way I won't know any of your personal information!
